<a id="p190"></a>
**[PDF p.190]** — DESCRIPTION · oil circuit

Oil circuit (diagram — image on [PDF p.190]): oil pan → oil strainer → oil pump →
(relief valve) → oil filter (relief valve) → oil cooler → main oil holes → crankshaft,
connecting rods, pistons & cylinders, cylinder head, camshaft journals, cams, valve
lifters & stems, camshaft drive/driven gears.

---
<a id="p191"></a>
**[PDF p.191]** — DESCRIPTION (cont.)

Oil from the oil pan is pumped up, passes through the oil filter, and is fed through oil
holes in the crankshaft and cylinder block, then returns by gravity to the oil pan. A
dipstick on the side of the oil pump body checks the oil level.

- **Oil pump** — trochoid type with a drive rotor and driven rotor; draws oil from the oil pan (via an oil strainer) and sends it under pressure to the engine.
- **Oil pressure regulator** — at high engine speed, prevents oversupply: a coil spring and valve keep the bypass closed until pressure gets too high, then the valve opens and excess oil returns to the oil pan.
- **Oil filter** — full-flow type with a paper element, mounted outside the engine. A relief valve ahead of the element opens if the element clogs, bypassing the filter so oil flows directly into the main oil hole.

**[PDF p.193]** — OIL PRESSURE CHECK

1. **CHECK ENGINE OIL QUALITY** — check for deterioration, water entry, discoloring or thinning. If poor, replace.
   - **(4A-FE for Europe & Australia, and 4A-GE):** use API grade **SE, SF, SG or better** + recommended viscosity.
   - **(4A-FE for Others):** use API grade **SD, SE, SF, SG or better** + recommended viscosity.
   - Recommended viscosity (SAE): by temperature range anticipated before the next oil change (chart on [PDF p.193]).
2. **CHECK ENGINE OIL LEVEL** — level should be between the "L" and "F" marks on the dipstick. If low, check for leaks and add oil to "F".
3. **REMOVE OIL PRESSURE SWITCH / SENDER GAUGE AND INSTALL OIL PRESSURE GAUGE**
   a. (4A-FE) Remove the alternator and bracket.
   b. Remove the oil pressure switch (4A-FE).
      > **HINT (w/ sender gauge):** use **SST 09027-12140**.
   c. Install an oil pressure gauge.
   d. (4A-FE) Reinstall the bracket and alternator.
4. **WARM UP ENGINE** to normal operating temperature.
5. **CHECK OIL PRESSURE**
   - **At idling: 0.3 kg/cm² (4.3 psi, 29 kPa) or more**
   - **At 3,000 rpm: 2.5 – 5.0 kg/cm² (36 – 71 psi, 245 – 490 kPa)**
6. **REMOVE OIL PRESSURE GAUGE**
   a. (4A-FE) Remove the alternator and bracket; remove the gauge.
   b. Apply adhesive to two or three threads of the oil pressure switch (4A-FE). **Adhesive: Part No. 08833-00080, THREE BOND 1344, LOCTITE 242 or equivalent.** *(Adaptor 1/8" × 28 BSPT, max tightening torque **10 N·m**.)*
   c. Reinstall the oil pressure switch; (4A-FE) reinstall bracket and alternator.
7. **START ENGINE AND CHECK FOR LEAKS.**

<a id="p194"></a>
**[PDF p.194]** — REPLACEMENT OF ENGINE OIL AND OIL FILTER

> **CAUTION:** Prolonged/repeated contact with mineral oil removes natural fats from the
> skin (dryness, irritation, dermatitis); used engine oil contains contaminants that may
> cause skin cancer. Use skin protection and washing facilities; wear oil-proof gloves;
> wash skin with soap and water (not gasoline/thinners/solvents). Dispose of used oil only
> at designated sites.

1. **DRAIN ENGINE OIL** — remove the oil filler cap; remove the drain plug and drain into a container.
2. **REPLACE OIL FILTER**
   a. Using **SST 09228-06500**, remove the oil filter (left side of the engine block).
   b. Inspect and clean the filter installation surface.
   c. Apply clean engine oil to the gasket of a new oil filter.

---
<a id="p195"></a>
**[PDF p.195]** — REPLACEMENT OF ENGINE OIL AND OIL FILTER (cont.)

2. (cont.) d. Lightly screw in the filter until you feel resistance. e. Then, using **SST 09228-06500**, tighten the filter an extra **3/4 turn**.
3. **REFILL WITH ENGINE OIL**
   a. Clean and install the drain plug with a new gasket. **Torque (drain plug): 15 N·m.**
   b. Fill with new oil, **API grade SD, SE, SF, SG or better**.
      **Capacity, 4A-FE — Drain and refill:**
      - Without oil filter change: **3.0 liters (3.1 US qts, 2.6 Imp. qts)**
      - With oil filter change: **3.2 liters (3.3 US qts, 2.8 Imp. qts)**

      <!-- NEEDS REVIEW: the Service Specifications section (A-2, not yet transcribed) prints slightly different capacities (w/o filter 3.0 L / 3.2 US qts; w/ filter 3.3 L / 3.5 US qts / 2.9 Imp). Reconcile the two when the spec section is transcribed. -->
   c. Reinstall the oil filler cap with the gasket.
4. **START ENGINE AND CHECK FOR LEAKS.**
5. **RECHECK ENGINE OIL LEVEL** (see [PDF p.193]).
