# Appendix — Service Specifications, SST and Index

This appendix is the master reference for the Toyota MR2 (AW11) covering both the
**4A-GE** and supercharged **4A-GZE** engines, the C52/E51 manual transaxles, and the
A240E/A241E automatic transaxles. It collects every service specification, standard-bolt
torque value, Special Service Tool (SST) and Special Service Material (SSM), the automatic
transmission hydraulic circuits, and the electrical wiring diagrams.

Notes on conventions used below:

- **Torque** is given in three columns — **kg-cm**, **ft-lb**, and **N·m** — exactly as
  printed. Small torque values are shown in `in.-lb` in the ft-lb column.
- **Resistance** uses **Ω** (ohm) and **kΩ** (kilohm).
- Multi-step tightening is shown as `1st / 2nd / 3rd` rows; a turn-angle step (e.g.
  `90° turns`) replaces a torque number for that step.
- The **"Index"** in this chapter's title refers to the wiring-diagram
  [System Index](#wiring-system-index) at the end — the manual has no separate
  alphabetical topic index.

## Electrical Wiring Diagrams

<a id="p1040"></a>
### How to Read This Section

**Pin number.** Connector pins are numbered in order from upper left to lower right, or
from upper right to lower left, depending on the connector. Male and female connectors are
distinguished by the shape of their internal pins.

- All connectors are shown from the open end, with the lock on top.
- When connectors with different numbers of terminals are used with the same parts, the
  pin number and the number of terminals are specified — e.g. `(1/4)` = No. 1 pin / 4-terminal connector.

**Junction block or relay block.** A wire passing through a junction/relay block is shown
as: *Wire Color* → *Block No.* → *Wire Color* (block inside).

**Wire color** — indicated by an alphabetical code:

| Code | Color | Code | Color |
| ---- | ----- | ---- | ----- |
| B | Black | LG | Light Green |
| BR | Brown | O | Orange |
| G | Green | P | Pink |
| GR | Gray | R | Red |
| L | Blue | V | Violet |
| W | White | Y | Yellow |

The 1st letter indicates the basic wire color and the 2nd indicates the stripe color.
Example: **R-G** indicates a Red wire with a Green stripe.

**Abbreviations used in the wiring diagrams:**

| Abbr. | Meaning | Abbr. | Meaning |
| ----- | ------- | ----- | ------- |
| A/C | Air Conditioner | M/T | Manual Transaxle |
| A/T | Automatic Transaxle | O/D | Overdrive |
| CB | Circuit Breaker | RH | Right-hand |
| ECU | Electronic Controlled Unit | S/W | Switch |
| EFI | Electronic Fuel Injection | T-VIS | Toyota Variable Induction System |
| FL | Fusible Link | VSV | Vacuum Switching Valve |
| J/B | Junction Block | w/ | With |
| LH | Left-hand | w/o | Without |
