# SST & Service Specifications

*Section code: `12` | PDF pages 313-335 of `source.pdf` | 23 pages | printed section pages 12-1 … 12-21*

> **This is the definitive torque/spec reference for the 18R / 18R-C / 18R-G engines.**
> Every torque, clearance, dimension, resistance and capacity below was transcribed
> **cell-by-cell from the page images** (the OCR text layer was severely garbled on the
> dense tables). Standard-bolt torque, the two engine "tightening torque for main parts"
> tables, and both engine service-specification tables are **image-verified**. A handful
> of cells contain apparent **misprints in the original manual** (e.g. an impossible
> unit conversion) — these are kept as printed per Rule 0 and flagged inline with
> `NEEDS REVIEW`. See [scripts/04_cleanup_methodology.md](../../../scripts/04_cleanup_methodology.md).
